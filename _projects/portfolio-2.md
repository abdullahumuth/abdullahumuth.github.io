This project was conducted as a research internship at Forschungszentrum Jülich, PGI-8, under the supervision of Dr. Markus Schmitt in Regensburg, Germany, from June 2024 to September 2024.

This project investigated mutual information as a proxy to predict the difficulty of learning wave functions using Neural Quantum States (NQS) in quantum many-body systems using transformers. I designed and implemented transformer architectures in Julia to estimate entropy and conditional entropy in discrete and continuous quantum systems. The work involved collaborating with a research team to enhance understanding of neural network representations of many-body quantum systems, applying scientific machine learning, information theory, and advanced statistical techniques.

**Key Technologies:** Julia, Transformers, Quantum Physics, Information Theory, Neural Networks
