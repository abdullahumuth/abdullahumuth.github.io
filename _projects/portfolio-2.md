**Research Intern** (June 2025 – Present)  
*Radboud University, High Energy Physics*  
*Supervisor: Prof. Sascha Caron*  
*Nijmegen, Netherlands*  

This project focuses on conducting research on foundation models for gravitational wave analysis using deep transformer-based symbolic regression. I am exploring state-of-the-art methods, including Multi-Modal Symbolic Regression (MMSR), for interpretable modeling of gravitational wave signals. The work combines advanced machine learning techniques with theoretical physics to develop better methods for analyzing gravitational wave data from detectors like LIGO and Virgo.

**Key Technologies:** Deep Learning, Transformers, Symbolic Regression, Python, Gravitational Wave Analysis
